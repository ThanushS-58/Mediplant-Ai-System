# 🌿 MediPlant AI – Preserving Traditional Wisdom Through Artificial Intelligence

> Discover. Identify. Preserve. Share.

MediPlant AI is an intelligent platform that combines **Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Community Knowledge Sharing** to identify medicinal plants and preserve valuable traditional healing knowledge for future generations.

## 🚀 What Makes MediPlant AI Special?

🔍 **Instant Plant Recognition**
- Upload a plant image and get real-time identification using AI.

🧠 **AI-Powered Analysis**
- Deep Learning models accurately recognize medicinal plants and provide confidence scores.

📚 **Traditional Knowledge Repository**
- Explore medicinal uses, preparation methods, cultural significance, and healing practices.

🌎 **Multilingual Support**
- Access information in multiple languages for wider accessibility.

🎙️ **Voice & Text Contributions**
- Share traditional knowledge through text or voice recordings.

👥 **Community-Driven Learning**
- Like, comment, contribute, and collaborate with other users and experts.

✅ **Expert Validation**
- Contributions are reviewed and verified to ensure authenticity and reliability.

🔊 **Accessible for Everyone**
- Built-in Text-to-Speech support for inclusive learning.
